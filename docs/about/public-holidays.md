# Public Holidays

As a Hong Kong–based company, we mainly follow the public holidays of **Chinese mainland** and **Hong Kong**. Our office is closed on these days. For our regular working hours, please see [About Us](about-us.md).

!!! info "Response time during holidays"
    During public holidays, our response may be slower than usual. Our tech and customer support teams will normally reply within **48 hours**.

    We ask for your understanding. We strive to deliver great products and services to our customers, while also supporting our team with a healthy work–life balance. Thank you for your patience.

!!! note "Substitution arrangements"
    As the Ching Ming Festival in 2026 falls on a Sunday, the following day (6 April) is designated as a general holiday in substitution. As that date coincides with Easter Monday, the next day that is not itself a general holiday (7 April) is observed as an additional general holiday.  
    As the Birthday of the Buddha and the Chung Yeung Festival in 2026 both fall on a Sunday, the day following each is designated as a general holiday in substitution.
