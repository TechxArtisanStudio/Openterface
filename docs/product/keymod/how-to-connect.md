# **How to Connect** | Setup Guide | Openterface KeyMod

## **Overview**

KeyMod connects your phone (running the KeyMod app) to a target device. Your phone acts as the keyboard and trackpad; KeyMod emulates HID input to the target.

![KeyMod Right Side](https://assets2.openterface.com/images/keymod/right-side.webp){:style="max-height:360px"}

**Connection flow:** Phone (KeyMod app) → KeyMod (USB or Bluetooth) → Target device

- **Phone/Tablet**: Requires the KeyMod controller app (Android or iPadOS)
- **KeyMod**: Connects to phone via USB or Bluetooth; connects to target via USB
- **Target Device**: No software installation required

## **What You Need**

- **KeyMod** — USB + Bluetooth HID emulator
- **Phone or tablet** — Android or iPadOS with KeyMod app installed
- **USB cable** — For KeyMod-to-target connection (included with KeyMod)
- **Optional**: Bluetooth for cable-free phone-to-KeyMod connection

## **Step-by-Step Setup**

### **Step 1 — Install the KeyMod App**

Download and install the KeyMod controller app on your Android device or iPad from the [App](/app) section.

### **Step 2 — Connect KeyMod to Your Phone**

**Option A: USB Connection**

Connect KeyMod to your phone via USB cable. The 2-in-1 variant fits both USB-A and USB-C ports; the USB C variant uses a USB-C cable.

![KeyMod Feature](https://assets2.openterface.com/images/keymod/feature.webp){:style="max-height:360px"}

**Option B: Bluetooth Connection**

Pair KeyMod with your phone via Bluetooth for a cable-free setup. Enable Bluetooth on your phone, then pair with KeyMod from the app.

![iPad Connect](https://assets2.openterface.com/images/keymod/ipad-connect.webp){:style="max-height:360px"}

### **Step 3 — Connect KeyMod to Target Device**

Plug KeyMod's USB connector into the target device (computer, kiosk, smart TV, set top box, etc.). The target will recognize KeyMod as a standard USB keyboard and mouse—no drivers or software needed.

![Windows Connect](https://assets2.openterface.com/images/keymod/windows-connect.webp){:style="max-height:360px"}

### **Step 4 — Start Controlling**

Open the KeyMod app on your phone. Use the on-screen keyboard and trackpad to control the target device. Configure profiles, macros, and hotkeys as needed.
