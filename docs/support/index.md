# Tech Support

We understand it's frustrating when things don't work as expected, and **we're here to help**. 
We recommend following these steps in order to find the quickest solution:

### 1. Check Documentation
Start by reviewing the product documentation for your specific device. Visit the product page for detailed guides, setup instructions, and feature information.

### 2. Check FAQs
Browse our FAQs for answers to common questions:

- **[General FAQs](/faq)**: General questions about KVM-over-USB and our products
- Product-specific FAQs are available on each product page

### 3. Search GitHub Issues or Community
Check existing discussions and reports:

- **[GitHub Issues](/app)**: Search for similar issues or bug reports
- **[Join Our Community](/community)**: Connect with our dev team and other users in forum-style discussions

### 4. Submit Troubleshooting Form
If you still need help, our [troubleshooting form](/support/minikvm) will guide you step-by-step to provide detailed information about your setup, hardware configuration, and the specific issue you're encountering. This helps our support team understand your situation and respond more effectively.

### 5. Email Support
If you prefer to email us directly, please note that **filling out the troubleshooting form is more effective** as it ensures we receive all the critical information needed to help you. However, you can always reach us at [support@openterface.com](mailto:support@openterface.com).

---

**Please be patient** as we work together to identify the cause and find the best solution. The more detailed information you provide, the faster we can help resolve your issue.
