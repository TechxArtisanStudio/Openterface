# **Welcome to the Openterface KVM-GO Beta Testing Program!**

**Dear Beta Testers,**

Thank you for joining the **Openterface KVM-GO** beta testing program! Your participation is vital to refining this next-generation, ultra-compact KVM-over-USB device before launch.

## **About KVM-GO**

The **KVM-GO** series (HDMI/DP/VGA versions) fits on your **keychain** and provides instant access to headless devices without monitors or network setups. It's the evolution of our Mini-KVM — **smaller, faster, and more versatile** — a true **grab-and-go tool** for critical tech moments.

## **How to Test**

### **1. Setup**

- Follow our Quick Start Guide: **openterface.com/product/kvm-go/how-to-connect**
- Test across different platforms (Windows, macOS, Linux, Android) and target devices (servers, mini-PCs, BIOS-level access)

### **2. Real-World Testing**

- Use KVM-GO in daily work or hobby projects
- Push video limits: different resolutions/refresh rates, HD/4K performance
- Test keyboard/mouse responsiveness and USB power stability

### **3. Report Bugs Clearly**
Include: app version, host OS/specs, target device, reproduction steps, expected vs actual results, screenshots/videos
